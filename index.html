Our Story

A Century of Heritage

For generations, Dominus Rooms has been a place where the soul of Dubrovnik is not merely admired — it is lived.

1887

The Beginning

Born from
Stone & Sea

"Built not for a night, but for a lifetime of memories."

Dominus Rooms was born from a deep love for Dubrovnik — its ancient limestone walls, its salt-kissed air, and the warm light that falls across the old city each evening like a blessing. What began as a family home within the heart of the UNESCO-protected Old Town has been carefully and lovingly transformed into a place where guests can experience the city the way it was meant to be lived.

Every stone in these walls has a story. The building dates back centuries, its thick Dalmatian limestone keeping the rooms cool in summer and warm in the quiet of winter. We have preserved every original detail — the arched doorways, the worn terracotta floors, the narrow staircase that winds upward toward the light — because we believe a place this old deserves to be treated with reverence.

Today

The Present

A Place to
Belong

"Dubrovnik is not just a destination. It is a feeling you carry home."

Today, Dominus Rooms offers a small number of carefully designed rooms, each one unique in shape and character but united by the same warmth and attention to detail. We are not a large hotel — and that is entirely by choice. Here, guests are welcomed as individuals, not room numbers. A familiar face at the door, a recommendation for the best table in the city, a quiet moment on the terrace as the sun sets over the Adriatic.

Dubrovnik has drawn travellers from across the world for centuries, and we consider it our honour to be the place they call home during their time here. Whether you are visiting for the first time or returning as so many of our guests do, Dominus Rooms offers something rare in travel — the feeling that you truly belong somewhere.
