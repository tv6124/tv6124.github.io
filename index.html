Our Story

A Century of Heritage

For over a century, Dominus Rooms has stood as a silent witness to the rise and fall of empires, the tides of history, and the enduring allure of the Adriatic coast.

1887

The Beginning

Born from
Stone & Sea

"Built not for a night, but for a lifetime of memories."

Dominus Rooms was born from a deep love for Dubrovnik — its ancient limestone walls, its salt-kissed air, and the warm light that falls across the old city each evening like a blessing. What began as a family home within the heart of the UNESCO-protected Old Town has been carefully and lovingly transformed into a place where guests can experience the city the way it was meant to be lived.

Every stone in these walls has a story. The building dates back centuries, its thick Dalmatian limestone keeping the rooms cool in summer and warm in the quiet of winter. We have preserved every original detail — the arched doorways, the worn terracotta floors, the narrow staircase that winds upward toward the light — because we believe a place this old deserves to be treated with reverence.

1960

The Golden Era

Preserving
the Original Soul

Within these walls, history was not replaced — it was carefully preserved.”

When Dominus Rooms was renewed, its most defining feature remained untouched: the original stone walls that have stood for generations. Rather than concealing them, the restoration celebrated their texture, craftsmanship, and story.

Each stone carries the character of the past, linking today’s guests with the building’s long history by the Adriatic coast. The design embraces this authenticity, pairing the historic masonry with warm modern comforts and light-filled interiors. By preserving the original walls, Dominus Rooms keeps its true identity intact — allowing visitors not only to stay in the hotel, but to experience a piece of its enduring story.
